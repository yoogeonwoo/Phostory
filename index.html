Our Journey

Transforming fleeting moments into timeless history.

Welcome to Phostory. We believe that every image is more than just a picture; it’s a living chapter of history waiting to be told. Our purpose is to provide you with an elegant and intuitive sanctuary where your most cherished memories can be archived as history and shared with the soul of the world.

Founded in 2026, Phostory is dedicated to offering a premium, contemporary experience where your memories don’t just exist—they truly shine.

Phostory is developed under the vision and support of Geonwoo Yoo, CEO of Lab. U. As an integral part of Lab. U, Phostory reflects our shared commitment to innovation and the preservation of human narratives.
